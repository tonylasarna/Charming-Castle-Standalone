Charming Castle Brand Guide

Luxurious, modern, and magical — inspired by rosy pink, gold accents, soft purple, white, and charcoal.

Logo usage

  • Keep clear space equal to the height of the monogram.
  • Use on light or softly textured backgrounds; pair with gold or charcoal.

Color palette

Rosy Pink #e95f93

Gold Accent #d0a85d

Soft Purple #d6c5f0

White #ffffff

Charcoal #2b2b31

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display — elegant and high-contrast.

Body: Inter — clean, legible, and modern.

Components

  • Buttons: pill shapes with gradient from rosy pink → gold → lavender; shadow glow.
  • Cards: glassmorphism with soft borders, rounded corners, and subtle gradient overlays.
  • Forms: rounded inputs, charcoal borders, generous spacing.

CTAs

Primary: Gradient pill; Secondary: charcoal outline on white.

Spacing

Use 8px grid with 16px base. Hero padding 64px+, section spacing 48px.

Image treatment

Soft glow around focal elements, dreamy blur gradients, and high-contrast subjects.
